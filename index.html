Interesting Facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are a part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts about Piegons

  • Piegons are incredibely complex and intelligent animals.
  • Piegons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Piegons have excellent hearing abilities

Interesting Facts about Camels

  • Camels ears are furry.
  • Camels can move easily across the sand because of their specially desgined feet.
  • Whenever camels find water they drink as much as they can.

Interesting Facts about Cicadas

  • Cicadas can survive high falls when they are babies.
  • Most cicadas eyes are red-orange.
  • Female cicadas are attracted to motors.

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  • A giant panda is much bigger than your teddy bear.
  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown).

Interesting Facts about Bears

  • Bears are extraordinarily intelligent animals.
  • Bears grieve deeply for others.
  • Bears have excellent senses of smell, sight and hearing.
